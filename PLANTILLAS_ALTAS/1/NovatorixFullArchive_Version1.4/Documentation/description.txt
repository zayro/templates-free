Novatorix - Business, Portfolio and Blog theme

WordPress Version: 2.9.2, 2.3.0, Columns: 2, Widget Ready: Yes, Functionality: CMS

Novatorix is a Wordpress Theme, best suited for Business and Portfolio sites. It comes with a fantastic 3D Image slideshow that can be controlled from your backend with a custom tool. The Theme has a huge wordpress custom backend (7 different option pages) that make customization of the Theme easy for those who dont know much about coding or wordpress.
