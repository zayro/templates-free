Novatorix is a Wordpress Theme, best suited for Business and Portfolio sites. 

It comes with a fantastic 3D Image slideshow that can be controlled from your backend 
with a custom tool. The Theme has a huge wordpress custom backend 
(7 different option pages) that make customization of the Theme easy for those who 
don’t know much about coding or wordpress.


Novatorix also has 10 Fantastic skins to choose from:

    Arsenic
    BurlyWood
    Chocolate
    DarkGreen
    FireBrick
    IvoryBlack
    PurpleTaupe
    SeaGreen
    SteelBlue
    Teal
